Welcome to Carento! Renting a car with us is easy, fast, and convenient. Whether you\'re going on a weekend getaway, need a car for business, or are planning a road trip, Carento makes renting a car seamless. Here’s how it works:

1. Browse & Select Your Car

  • Browse Our Fleet: Start by browsing our diverse fleet of cars. Whether you\'re looking for a compact car, an SUV, or a luxury vehicle, we have a variety of options to suit your needs.
  • Filter Your Choices: Use our search filters to select the type of vehicle, pick-up location, rental dates, and any additional features you may need, such as GPS or a child car seat.
  • Check Availability: View real-time availability and pricing for your selected car. If your desired car is unavailable, you’ll see alternative options.

2. Make a Reservation

  • Select Your Rental Period: Choose the dates and times you need the car, from daily rentals to weekly or monthly bookings.
  • Add Extras: You can add optional extras such as additional drivers, insurance, or GPS systems at this stage.
  • Review Your Booking: Review your booking details, including the car type, rental period, and total cost.
  • Secure Payment: Confirm your reservation by entering your payment details through our secure payment gateway. We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and payment systems like PayPal.
  • Get Confirmation: Once your payment is processed, you\'ll receive an instant email confirmation with all the details of your reservation, including a unique booking reference number.

3. Pick Up Your Car

  • Locate Your Pick-Up Location: You’ll find your car ready for you at the designated pick-up location. We offer several convenient locations, including major airports, city centers, and train stations.
  • Present Your ID & License: When you arrive, we’ll need to verify your identity. Please bring a valid driver’s license and the credit card used for the booking.
  • Car Inspection: Before you drive off, we will conduct a quick inspection of the car with you, noting any pre-existing damage. You’ll also receive instructions on how to operate the vehicle, including key features and emergency procedures.
  • Sign the Rental Agreement: Sign the rental agreement, which will outline the terms and conditions of your rental. If you have any questions about the agreement, our staff is happy to assist you.

4. Enjoy Your Ride

  • Drive with Confidence: Once you’re all set, you’re free to enjoy your trip! Feel free to use the car for your travel needs, whether it\'s for business, leisure, or exploration.
  • 24/7 Roadside Assistance: If you encounter any issues during your rental, we offer 24/7 roadside assistance to help with breakdowns, flat tires, or emergencies.
  • Fuel Policy: Our cars are provided with a full tank of fuel. You can return the vehicle with a full tank or pay for the fuel used during your rental period. Please note, if the car is returned with less than a full tank, you may be charged a refueling fee.

5. Return the Car

  • Return Location: Return the car to the same location where you picked it up, or select a different return location if available. For airport rentals, simply drop the car off at the designated Carento area.
  • Car Inspection & Check-Out: Upon return, we will conduct a brief inspection of the vehicle to ensure there’s no damage or additional issues. Please remember to return the car on time to avoid any late fees.
  • Final Payment (If Necessary): Any remaining balance (such as extra mileage charges or fuel costs) will be settled at the time of return. You can pay with your credit/debit card or any other available payment method.
  • Get Your Final Receipt: Once everything is checked, we\’ll send you a final receipt by email, outlining the charges and any deposits returned. If applicable, we’ll also issue any refunds for unused services.

6. Enjoy Our Loyalty Program (Optional)

  • Earn Points with Every Rental: If you\'re a member of the Carento Loyalty Program, you can earn points with every booking to redeem for discounts, free upgrades, and other special offers.
  • Exclusive Deals & Offers: Sign up for exclusive offers, early access to new fleet options, and seasonal promotions.
